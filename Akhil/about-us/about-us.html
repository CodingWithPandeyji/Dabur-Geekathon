Who We Are - About Dabur

Transforming Living Of Every Household Through Ayurveda

A lot has happened over the past 138 years. We’ve seen our good intentions grow into the World’s Largest Ayurveda Company. From a family business with humble beginnings to a transnational enterprise with products in over 120 countries across the globe. The love we have been given, has honestly brought us a long way. Today, Dabur India has a portfolio of over 250 Herbal and Ayurvedic products. We’re present in Hair Care, Oral Care, Health Care, Skin Care, Home Care and Food & Beverages. But what we’re most happy about, is being in your good company.

LEADERSHIP

The Legacy behind Dabur

What started with a dream of our founder Dr. S. K. Science of Ayurveda

Dabur chooses the path of nurture. Backed by extensive research and development. Originating nearly 5,000 years ago, Ayurvedic texts were researched by Dabur in its quest for natural remedies. Today, its application in modern life has been renewed through the scientific research and validation undertaken at Dabur.
